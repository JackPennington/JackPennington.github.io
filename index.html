About Me

Hello, welcome to my personal website. I am 19 years old and live in Wigan. I attended Highfield St Matthews Primary School before going to Abraham Guest Academy to eventually doing my A-levels at St John Rigby College. Now I currently am employed on a Higher Degree Apprenticeship as a Business Analyst with Shop Direct who own Very, Littlewoods and Very Exclusive. Whilst working I also study Digital and Technology Solutions at Manchester Metropolitan University. I live in a family of 6 people, my Mum, Dad and 3 brothers. I have recently purchased and currently drive a Volkswagen Golf TSI. I support Wigan Athletic FC (UP THE TICS!) and I go watching the home or away matches with my mates, I also enjoy watching any league of football. I occasionally enjoy playing golf in the summer.
