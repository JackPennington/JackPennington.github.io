About Me

Hello, welcome to my personal website. I am 19 years old and live in Wigan. I attended Highfield St Matthews Primary School before going to Abraham Guest Academy to eventually doing my A-levels at St John Rigby College. I am currently employed on a Higher Degree Apprenticeship as a Business Analyst with Shop Direct who are responsible for Very, Littlewoods and Very Exclusive. Whilst working I also study Digital and Technology Solutions at Manchester Metropolitan University. My family comprises 6 people including my Mum, Dad, 3 brothers and myself. I currently drive a Volkswagen Golf TSI and I support Wigan Athletic FC (UP THE TICS!). I attend home and away matches with friends. In fact I enjoy watching football of any standard. I occasionally enjoy playing golf in the summer.

Work Experience

My work experience started when I was just 14. My first job was to referee football games on a Saturday. The pay was amazing! I was responsible on average for two games per week. I landed a part time job at Clarks Shoes. I spent a short while there and was awarded employee of the month the first month I was there! I then moved onto working at Dominos, where I was working with my mates. Two years passed, majority of the time working in-store making pizzas. The last 9 months spent delivering pizzas when I got my first car. I currently work for Shop Direct as a Business Analyst, working on Very.co.uk, Very Exclusive, Littlewoods & Littlewoods Ireland whilst attending Manchester Metropolitan University studying Digital & Technology Solutions.
